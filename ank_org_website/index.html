Welcome to AutoNetkit.


AutoNetkit allows you to create and deploy large-scale emulated networks based on the Netkit emulation platform. It also has support for Junosphere, C-BGP, and limited support for GNS3/Dynagen.


AutoNetkit is Open-Source and available under a BSD licence.

More information is available at the documentation website.

This project was supported by the Australian Government through an Australian Postgraduate Award and Australian Research Council Discovery Grants DP110103505 and DP0985063; and by the University of Adelaide.
